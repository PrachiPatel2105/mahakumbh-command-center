## 🎨 Design Guidelines

### Color Scheme
Use colors from your dashboard for consistency:
- **Primary:** Orange (#EA580C)
- **Background:** Dark slate (#0F172A, #1E293B)
- **Accent:** Blue, Green, Red (for status indicators)
- **Text:** White and light gray

### Typography
- **Headings:** Bold, large, easy to read
- **Body:** Clear, concise bullet points
- **Code/Tech:** Monospace font

### Images
- **High Quality:** Use full-resolution screenshots
- **Annotations:** Highlight key features with arrows/circles
- **Consistency:** Similar styling across all images

### Layout
- **Minimal:** Don't overcrowd slides
- **Visual Hierarchy:** Important info stands out
- **White Space:** Give content room to breathe
- **Consistency:** Same layout template throughout

## 💡 Presentation Tips

### Content Tips:
- One key message per slide
- Use bullet points, not paragraphs
- Include visuals on every slide
- Tell a story: Problem → Solution → Impact

### Design Tips:
- Consistent font sizes
- High contrast for readability
- Professional color scheme
- Quality over quantity

### Delivery Tips (if presenting):
- Start strong with the problem
- Show, don't just tell (use screenshots)
- Highlight the innovation
- End with clear impact and next steps
