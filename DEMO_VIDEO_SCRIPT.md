### [2:40 - 3:40] Feature Demo #2: Crowd Density Map (60 seconds)

**[Scroll to crowd density map]**

> "Now, this is one of my favorite features - the interactive crowd density map.
>
> **[Hover over different zones]**
> Each card represents one of the twelve operational zones. You can see the zone name, current crowd count versus capacity, and an occupancy percentage bar.
>
> The color coding is important: green border means safe, yellow means warning level, and red indicates critical occupancy.
>
> **[Point to specific zones]**

>
> **[Click "Start Live Simulation" button]**
> And here's something unique - I've built a live simulation mode. Watch the numbers change in real-time as the system simulates crowd movement.
>
> **[Let it run for 10 seconds, show numbers changing]**
> See how the occupancy percentages update, status colors change, and the progress bars animate? This is perfect for training operators or testing response scenarios.
>
> **[Stop simulation]**
> This gives event managers powerful situational awareness of crowd distribution across the entire venue."

## 💡 Pro Tips

### Do's:
✅ Speak clearly and at a moderate pace  
✅ Show enthusiasm (you built something cool!)  
✅ Point to features as you explain them  
✅ Let animations complete (don't rush)  
✅ Mention the live URL clearly  
✅ Emphasize the AI-assisted development  
✅ Show the simulation feature (it's unique!)  
✅ Keep it under 7 minutes
